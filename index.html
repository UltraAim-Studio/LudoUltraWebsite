Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play with friends?

Create a private room, share the room code with your friends, and they can join instantly. You can also challenge specific friends from your contact list.

Is internet required?

Internet is required for online multiplayer modes. However, you can play locally using the Pass & Play mode without an internet connection.

Can I play against the computer?

Yes! We offer AI opponents with adjustable difficulty levels. Play as many games as you want to hone your skills.

How do I customize my characters?

Visit the character customization section in your profile. Unlock new characters through gameplay, achievements, and the in-game store.

Is the game free?

Yes! Ludo Ultra 3D is completely free to play with optional in-game purchases for cosmetics and battle pass items.

Where can I report issues?

Contact our support team through the in-game help menu or email us at support@ludoultra3d.com
